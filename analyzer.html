How it works

For each supplier the tool computes:

1. Topline score — topline_risk_score (0–1)

2. Composite sub-score — average of country, industry, purchase, and trade-partner risk scores (0–1)

3. Gap = |topline − composite|

Rows where the gap exceeds the threshold are flagged. Alerts and regulations are surfaced to help distinguish legitimate discrepancies from algorithm errors.
